Wall-E v 2.7

Wall-E is a two-wheeled educational self balancing and line following robot developed by SRA-VJTI, designed to teach embedded robotics concepts to students . taught and handled a crowd of 220+ students during the workshop still one of the best weekends i have had . we manufactured and tested over 60 kits for the event it was crazy fun!

Proximity

Proximity is a reinforcement learning project designed to train autonomous agents to play Trackmania using a custom Proximal Policy Optimization (PPO) architecture. Built upon the Linesight environment, it focuses on fine-tuning discrete control policies to master a given map. The repository features a modular pipeline for experimenting with reward shaping, hyperparameter optimization, and real-time training metrics.

ESP32-fc

esp32-fc is a project focused on developing a flight controller for drones using the ESP32 microcontroller. The project aims to provide a low-cost, open-source solution for drone enthusiasts and researchers. It features a modular design, allowing for easy integration of various sensors and components, as well as support for advanced flight modes and autonomous navigation.
